## Why Sponsor?

Sonic Therapy is not a CRUD demo. It is a production-grade platform built on domain-specific audio science:

- Brainwave entrainment (binaural beat generation and 3-phase frequency ramping)
- Solfeggio healing frequencies (174–963 Hz)
- Cultural healing modes (Chinese Five-Element, Indian Raga Chikitsa, Ottoman Maqam)
- Multi-model AI music generation with automatic provider fallback
- Full product surface: auth, billing, storage, evaluation, and a studio dashboard

This is original implementation from research literature — not a library wrapper.
